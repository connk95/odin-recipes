Banana Chocolate Muffins

Description

The best and easiest banana chocolate chip muffins you can easily make from home!

  • 4 bananas
  • 75g butter
  • 155ml white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 5ml vanilla
  • 5ml baking soda
  • salt
  • 350ml flour
  • 200g chocolate

Steps

  1. Mash ripe bananas in mixing bowl with softened butter
  2. Add sugar, vanilla, egg, mix
  3. Add 1 pinch salt, baking soda, mix
  4. Add flour, chocolate
  5. Cook 20 minutes at 175C
