Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

A classic peanut butter and jelly sandwatch when you're short on time

Ingredients

  • Serves 1
  • 2 slices bread
  • peanut butter
  • raspberry jelly

Steps

  1. spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. spread jelly on second slice of bread
  3. fold two slices of bread together to make sandwich
