Chorizo Egg Toast

Description

Deliciously savoury and spicy chorizo and poached egg toast

Ingredients

  • Serves 2
  • 1 chorizo sausage
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices bread
  • 8 mini tomato
  • (optional) parmesan cheese
  • (optional) cilantro
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Crumble and pan fry 1 chorizo sausage, remove from heat
  2. Gently fry halved mini tomatoes in sausage fat
  3. Toast 2 slices bread in oven
  4. Poach 2 eggs, 3 minutes each
  5. Add eggs, chorizo, tomatoes, and optional cheese and cilantro to toast, add salt & pepper to taste
